How to Use Eyevoke

1. Start Communication

Navigate to the 'Start Communication' section to begin your session. The interface is designed for immediate accessibility.

2. Smart Auto-Scroll

Experience hands-free reading. The text scrolls automatically when you focus, adapting to your reading pace.

3. Blink Selection

Perform a long blink to select text or trigger actions. Our advanced detection algorithm distinguishes natural blinks from commands.

4. Personalized Adjustments

Configure scroll speed and blink sensitivity in the Settings menu to match your comfort level perfectly.

5. Custom Sentences

Add your own custom sentences for quick access. Tailor the experience to your daily communication needs.

About Eyevoke

Eyevoke is a blink-based communication interface designed to assist individuals with limited motor or speech abilities.

By converting intentional eye blinks into meaningful selections, Eyevoke enables hands-free communication without relying on speech, touch, or external hardware.

Who is Eyevoke for?

Eyevoke is built for people with motor disabilities, temporary injuries, neurological conditions, or anyone who benefits from alternative communication methods.

Why Eyevoke?

  • No external hardware required
  • Privacy-focused, on-device interaction
  • Customizable blink and scroll settings
  • Designed with accessibility-first principles
